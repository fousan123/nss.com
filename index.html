National Service Scheme

The National Service Scheme is an Indian government sector public service program conducted by the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports of the Government of India. Popularly known as NSS, the scheme was launched in Gandhiji's Centenary year in 1969.

NOT ME BUT YOU official webpage

Mission

The mission of NSS is to develop the personality and character of students through voluntary community service. NSS strives to foster social and national development by involving students in various social service activities that contribute to the welfare of the community while enhancing their personal growth and leadership skills.

Coming works

PRABHA

Advocating for Physically Challenged Students and Ensuring Government Support

In the tapestry of our society, the threads of diversity and inclusion must intertwine seamlessly, recognizing and embracing the unique strengths of every individual, regardless of their physical abilities.Actions to raise the issues of physically challenged students and others at the government level and provide necessary support.

HARITHAM

Promoting Ecological Balance and Sustainable Lifestyles: A Holistic Project Approach

The project's primary objective is to uphold ecological equilibrium and foster sustainable lifestyles through a variety of engaging activities. By concentrating on habitat restoration, renewable energy adoption, waste reduction campaigns, and community education.

BAVANAM

Empowering Communities: Facilitating Affordable Housing for Economically Backward Individuals

a project dedicated to realizing the dreams of economically disadvantaged individuals: the endeavor to provide affordable housing. This project stands as a testament to the unwavering commitment to uplift those facing financial barriers from the shadows of insecurity into the embrace of stability.
